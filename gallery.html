Apr 2026
CEEECF joined fellow NGOs and the dedicated staff of the American University of Nigeria at an inspiring Earth Day 2026 commemoration. Yola
Expanding the Nigerian Earth Warriors network, we launched an Eco-Club at Rochas Foundation Secondary School. The event featured interactive climate education using board games, an official induction, and a campus tree-planting exercise.

School Eco-Club Creation

Yola
In partnership with ActionAid and the Nigerian Earth Warriors, we successfully launched a new Eco-Club at the Remi Educational Foundation. The initiative included interactive climate education, student induction pledges, and a practical tree planting exercise to instill environmental stewardship.

Climate Justice Walk

Yola
As part of the Global Week of Climate Actions, we mobilized over 200 young people to march through Yola under the "Young People for Climate Action Now" banner. The solidarity walk demanded climate finance accountability and a clear phase-out of fossil fuels.

Regenerative Agriculture Session

Girei
A hands-on training module for local farmers facing top-soil depletion. We demonstrated rapid composting, crop rotation strategies, and conservation tillage techniques to restore soil health and improve long-term yield viability.
