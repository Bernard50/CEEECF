Mitigation Techniques and Climate Action

Supporting community-based strategies to reduce emissions, adaptive capacity, and strengthen climate resilience.

Our Approach

Climate change is an existential threat that requires immediate, community-led action. We focus on integrating scientific knowledge with indigenous practices to create mitigation strategies that are both effective and culturally appropriate.

Our focus extends beyond just emissions; we aim to build the internal capacity of communities to adapt to the changing climate while protecting their natural heritage.

Renewable Energy

Promoting the adoption of solar, wind, and clean cooking solutions to reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

Ecosystem Restoration

Implementing reforestation and wetland protection projects to enhance natural carbon sinks.

Key Objectives

  • check_circle Reduce local carbon footprints
  • check_circle Strengthen disaster preparedness
  • check_circle Influence climate-resilient policy
