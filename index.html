Building resilience through innovative community-led solutions.

To promote environmental sustainability and community resilience through awareness creation, capacity building, and innovative solutions that address environmental degradation, enhance climate adaptation, and foster peace, protection, and inclusive development.

Infrastructure

Energy Transparency Initialization

Problem Systematic estimated billing and degraded power infrastructure impacted regional economic stability.
Approach Coordinated analytical demands at utility headquarters for immediate transparency and metered distribution.
Outcome Formal commitments secured for immediate network repairs and transitional metering.

Community

Wuro Chekke Grid Analysis

Problem Artificial grid blackouts severely restricted daily operations for fully metered residents and micro-businesses.
Approach Structured participatory assessment to document the empirical economic impact.
Outcome Data-driven framework established to negotiate equitable distribution schedules.

MOVE Structural Coalition

Problem Fragmented organizational resources limited the scale of youth-led systemic interventions.
