Support FAQ

Is my donation tax-deductible?

expand_more

YeS, CEEECF is a registered non-profit organization. You will receive a tax receipt via email immediately after your donation.

Can I donate in other currencies?

expand_more

While our primary account is in local currency, we accept international transfers. Most banks will automatically handle the conversion at their standard daily rate. Please consult your bank for specific fees regarding international wire transfers.

How do I cancel a monthly donation?

expand_more

You can manage or cancel your monthly giving at any time through the link provided in your initial confirmation email, or by contacting our support team.
